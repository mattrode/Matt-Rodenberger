About Me

My name is Matt and I currently work for the City of Philadelphia. I am primarily working on a project involving the mass cleanup of Parcel data in the City. This position offers the unique opportunity being the center of all GIS data management between city departments and how different analysts use spatial data in their day-to-day jobs. In my free time I love sports, I play hockey, and have quite the taste for craft beers.
